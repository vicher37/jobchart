How JobCharted came into being

Hi there! As a student looking for a job, I wonder what is the best way to find the company that 'fits' me. I want to know more than job descriptions or official company introduction. I want personalized reviews, but reading through thousands of reviews on traditional job websites (Indeed, Glassdoor) takes too much time for me.

Is there a way to reflect the sentiment in reviews, and present a quantitative yet personalized view of the company? There is no such thing on the internet yet according to my research, so I created JobCharted.
